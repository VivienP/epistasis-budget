# TrpB 650M result — structural allocation

**Status.** The map-recovery and downstream profiles are complete and conform to the registered TrpB
recipe. The compact evidence artifact is tracked, but the result remains provisional. GB1 and TrpB are
decided separately.

## Question

Given the same plate budget, which selection rule produces the most informative measurements?

- `fitness` spends the budget on variants with the highest zero-shot ESM score.
- `random` samples the same candidate universe uniformly.
- `structural` covers as many pairwise and third-order interaction loops as possible.
- `info` weights that loop coverage by ESM masking-perturbation dispersion.

## Map recovery

On TrpB, `info` satisfies the frozen pairwise rule against both `fitness` and `random`. The ablation
changes the interpretation: `structural` exceeds `info` at budgets 96 and 192 for both Pearson and
Spearman recovery. The supported contribution is loop coverage, not the masking-dispersion weight.

The full decision-bearing point estimates are stored in
[`structural_allocation_650m.json`](../../artifacts/structural_allocation_650m.json). Pairwise and
third-order results remain separate; the pooled metric is not used for the decision.

## Downstream impact

The downstream benchmark asks whether the selected plate trains a fixed pairwise-ridge learner to rank
held-out double and triple mutants. Selection cannot see measured fitness. The learner receives labels
only after the plate is fixed and never receives held-out ESM features.

- On TrpB, structural selection beats fitness-greedy in 20/20 partitions, with a mean
  `S_macro` learning-curve AUC difference of +0.286.
- The ESM-dispersion contrast is positive in 0/20 partitions at B=192, with mean difference −0.025.
- On GB1, the corresponding structural gate also passes, while the ESM-dispersion gate does not.

The result therefore transfers the same practical conclusion across two biochemically different
four-site landscapes: covering interaction structure produces a better training plate than selecting
variants by predicted fitness. It does not establish generality beyond these landscapes or this fixed
downstream learner.

## Evidence boundary

- TrpB uses the assayed Tm9D8* parent `VFVS` at positions 183, 184, 227, and 228.
- The candidate universe contains singles, doubles, and triples over the full 20-letter alphabet.
- The source mirror contains 871 imputed fitness values that are not identified row by row.
- The map-recovery estimator retains an ESM prior for unmeasured loop members. The downstream primary
  learner does not use that prior.
- The original corrective GB1 map-recovery decision remains `inconclusive_zero_gpu`; the TrpB result
  does not replace it.
